Stealing a review from [Andrew Blackman](https://andrewblackman.net/2025/03/perfection-vincenzo-latronico-review/):

> Perfection is not satisfying in the way that novels are traditionally
> satisfying—if you want to feel an emotional investment in the lives of Anna and
> Tom and to be gripped by their story, you’ll be disappointed. But if you want a
> sharp take on the emptiness of contemporary life, it delivers in spades.
